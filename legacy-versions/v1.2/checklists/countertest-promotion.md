# Countertest Promotion Checklist

- The tested claim is stated explicitly.
- The formal or static reason for failure is documented.
- The input passes the validator and the independent statement scan.
- A trusted oracle, checker, or proof supports WA claims.
- TLE/MLE claims use direct-redirection logs and judge-relevant assumptions.
- Local artifacts are labeled as local artifacts, not official counters.
- If the best result is only stress, report it as stress and do not overclaim.
- The final folder contains input, output when appropriate, logs, and an explanation.
